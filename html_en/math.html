Sets

Basic Concepts

Set: a whole made up of elements that satisfy certain conditions.
Representation methods: enumeration, description
Operations: \(\cup\) (Union), \(\cap\) (Intersection), \(A \setminus B\) (Complement)
Example: \( A = \{1, 2, 3\}, B = \{2, 3, 4\} \), Find \( A \cap B \).
Analysis: \( A \cap B = \{2, 3\} \), take the common elements.

üìà
Functions

Definitions and Properties

Function: \( y = f(x) \), mapping from domain to range.
Properties: monotonicity, parity, periodicity
Example: Determine the parity of \( f(x) = x^2 \).
Analysis: \( f(-x) = (-x)^2 = x^2 = f(x) \), an even function.

üßÆ
Exponential Functions

Forms and Properties

Form: \( y = a^x \ (a > 0, a \neq 1) \)
Properties: when \( a > 1 \), monotonically increasing; when \( 0 < a < 1 \), monotonically decreasing.
Example: Find the value of \( y = 2^x \) at \( x = 3 \).
Analysis: \( y = 2^3 = 8 \).

üî¢
Logarithmic Functions

Forms and Properties

Form: \( y = \log_a x \ (a > 0, a \neq 1) \)
Properties: domain \( (0, +\infty) \), range \( \mathbb{R} \)
Example: Find \( \log_2 8 \).
Analysis: \( \log_2 8 = \log_2 (2^3) = 3 \).

üìê
Trigonometric Functions

Basic Formulas

Definitions: \( \sin \theta, \cos \theta, \tan \theta \)
Formula: \( \sin^2 \theta + \cos^2 \theta = 1 \)
Example: Find \( \sin \frac{\pi}{6} \).
Analysis: \( \sin \frac{\pi}{6} = \frac{1}{2} \).

üîç
Sequences

Arithmetic and Geometric

Arithmetic: \( a_n = a_1 + (n-1)d \)
Geometric: \( a_n = a_1 \cdot q^{n-1} \)
Example: Arithmetic sequence with first term 2, common difference 3, find the 5th term.
Analysis: \( a_5 = 2 + (5-1) \cdot 3 = 14 \).

Vectors

Operations and Properties

Operations: addition, scalar product \( \vec{a} \cdot \vec{b} = |\vec{a}||\vec{b}|\cos \theta \)
Example: \( \vec{a} = (1, 2), \vec{b} = (3, 4) \), find \( \vec{a} + \vec{b} \).
Analysis: \( \vec{a} + \vec{b} = (1+3, 2+4) = (4, 6) \).

Basic Inequalities

Mean Value and Cauchy

Mean value: \( \frac{a + b}{2} \geq \sqrt{ab} \)
Example: Verify for \( a = 4, b = 9 \).
Analysis: \( \frac{4 + 9}{2} = 6.5 \geq \sqrt{4 \cdot 9} = 6 \), holds true.

üìâ
Quadratic Functions

Forms and Properties

Standard form: \( y = ax^2 + bx + c \)
Vertex form: \( y = a(x - h)^2 + k \)
Vertex: \( \left( -\frac{b}{2a}, f\left(-\frac{b}{2a}\right) \right) \)
Example: Find the vertex of \( y = x^2 - 4x + 3 \).
Analysis: \( x = -\frac{-4}{2 \cdot 1} = 2 \), \( y = 2^2 - 4 \cdot 2 + 3 = -1 \), vertex \( (2, -1) \).

üîÑ
Permutations & Combinations

Basic Formulas

Permutation: \( P(n, k) = \frac{n!}{(n-k)!} \)
Combination: \( C(n, k) = \frac{n!}{k!(n-k)!} \)
Example: Select 3 people from 5, find the number of combinations.
Analysis: \( C(5, 3) = \frac{5!}{3! \cdot 2!} = 10 \).

Solid Geometry

Basic Concepts

Point, line, plane relationships: parallel, perpendicular, angle
Example: Find the diagonal length of a cube (side length \( a \)).
Analysis: Space diagonal \( d = a\sqrt{3} \), derived from Pythagorean theorem.

üìè
Linear Equations

Forms and Properties

Forms: point-slope \( y - y_1 = k(x - x_1) \), general \( ax + by + c = 0 \)
Example: Find the equation of the line passing through \( (1, 2) \) with slope 3.
Analysis: \( y - 2 = 3(x - 1) \), so \( y = 3x - 1 \).

Circle Equations

Standard and General Forms

Standard form: \( (x - h)^2 + (y - k)^2 = r^2 \)
Example: Center \( (1, -2) \), radius 3, find the equation.
Analysis: \( (x - 1)^2 + (y + 2)^2 = 9 \).

Ellipse

Standard Equation

Standard form: \( \frac{x^2}{a^2} + \frac{y^2}{b^2} = 1 \ (a > b > 0) \)
Example: Find the focal distance of the ellipse \( \frac{x^2}{16} + \frac{y^2}{9} = 1 \).
Analysis: \( c = \sqrt{a^2 - b^2} = \sqrt{16 - 9} = \sqrt{7} \).

Complex Numbers

Operations and Properties

Form: \( z = a + bi \)
Operations: addition, subtraction, multiplication, division
Modulus: \( |z| = \sqrt{a^2 + b^2} \)
Example: Find \( (1 + i)(2 - i) \).
Analysis: \( = 2 - i + 2i - i^2 = 3 + i \).

Statistics & Probability

Basic Concepts

Probability: \( P(A) = \frac{\text{favorable outcomes}}{\text{total outcomes}} \)
Example: Roll a die, find the probability of getting 6.
Analysis: \( P = \frac{1}{6} \).

üìä
Derivatives

Definition and Applications

Definition: \( f'(x) = \lim_{\Delta x \to 0} \frac{f(x + \Delta x) - f(x)}{\Delta x} \)
Example: Find the derivative of \( f(x) = x^3 \).
Analysis: \( f'(x) = 3x^2 \).

Integration

Basic Concepts

Indefinite integral: \( \int f(x) \, dx = F(x) + C \)
Example: Find \( \int x^2 \, dx \).
Analysis: \( \int x^2 \, dx = \frac{x^3}{3} + C \).

üß≠
Space Vectors

Operations and Applications

Operations: scalar product, vector product
Example: Find the scalar product of \( \vec{a} = (1, 0, 1), \vec{b} = (0, 1, 0) \).
Analysis: \( \vec{a} \cdot \vec{b} = 1 \cdot 0 + 0 \cdot 1 + 1 \cdot 0 = 0 \).

Reasoning and Proof

Methods

Methods: mathematical induction, proof by contradiction
Example: Prove \( 1 + 3 + \dots + (2n-1) = n^2 \).
Analysis: Mathematical induction: for \( n = 1 \), holds; assume for \( n = k \) holds, then verify for \( n = k+1 \).

üíª
Algorithm Basics

Basic Concepts

Algorithm: methods and steps to solve problems
Example: Write an algorithm to find \( 1 + 2 + \dots + n \).
Analysis: Use a loop, or formula \( S = \frac{n(n+1)}{2} \).
